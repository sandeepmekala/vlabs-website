Procedure :

  1. Make the connections as shown in the circuit diagram.

  2. Switch the circuit on by plugging the key..

  3. Adjust the rheostat to pass a small current through the given resistor.

  4. Note the ammeter and voltmeter readings.

  5. Increase the current step-by-step by adjusting the rheostat and take about 4 to 6 sets of readings. Note carefully ammeter and voltmeter readings in each set.

  6. Tabulate the observations as given in the observation table.

  7. Find V/I ratio  in each of observations and record it in the observation table.

  8. Draw a graph by plotting voltmeter readings(V)  along the X-axis and the corresponding along the ammeter readings(I) Y-axis as shown in the diagram below.
